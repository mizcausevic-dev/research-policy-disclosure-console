# Kinetic Gain Embedded Tie-Back

`research-policy-disclosure-console` is the Higher Education / University Ops proof surface for research disclosures, missing packet proof, stale training evidence, and review-safe policy posture.

## What it demonstrates

- disclosure intake and routing posture
- owner-safe escalation for missing COI, IRB, and training proof
- committee readiness through packet completeness visibility
- operator-readable review workflows without exposing live campus systems

## Why this matters to Kinetic Gain Embedded

Embedded products increasingly need policy-aware and evidence-aware operator surfaces inside customer workflows. This repo shows the reusable primitive:

1. ingest structured project and packet exports
2. analyze missing proof, stale packets, and workflow drift
3. present one buyer-readable control surface for operators, reviewers, and administrators

The same architecture can be embedded into SaaS products that need:

- disclosure workflow visibility
- committee and review-readiness analytics
- packet-completeness scoring
- safe internal review tooling for university compliance operations
